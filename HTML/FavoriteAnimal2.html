Slowpoke is a lazy creature that fishes, using its tail as a lure for the fish.
As said, Slowpoke is rather slow and lazy. However, it does not feel pain when its tail is bitten.
When its tail is bitten by a Shellder, it evolves into its evolved form of Slowbro.
