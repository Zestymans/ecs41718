Potato Pie

How to bake a potato pie

Part 1: Ingredients

  • 1⁄3 cup butter or 1⁄3 cup margarine
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3⁄4 cup evaporated milk
  • 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ ground cinnamon

How to play Nuts About Mao

Part 1: Setup

Step 1: Obtain a deck of playing cards and remove the
jacks and jokers from the deck. Set them aside.
Step 2: Shuffle the deck of cards without the jokers and jacks.
Step 3: Take the jacks and jokers and place them on the table.
There should be one jack or joker for every player except one.
Step 4: Deal each player a hand of six cards.

Step 5: Begin play according to the rules.
