Potato Pie

How to bake a potato pie

Part 1: Ingredients

  • 1⁄3 cup butter or 1⁄3 cup margarine
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3⁄4 cup evaporated milk
  • 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 9 inches unbaked pie shells

Ready in 1 hour 10 minutes
Serves 6-8 people

Directions

Step 1: Put butter and sugar in a mixing bowl.
Step 2: Add the eggs and mix the eggs, butter and sugar well.
Step 3: Add the milk, sweet potatoes, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
Step 4: Mix the resulting mixture well and pour it into a pie shell.
Step 5: Bake the pie at 425 degrees for 15 minutes.
Step 6: Reduce the heat to 350 degrees and bake 35-40 minutes longer or until the pie seems done.
Step 7: Cool the pie by putting it in a refrigerator
